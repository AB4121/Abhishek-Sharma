About Me

Who I Am

I am a PhD scholar at IIT Indore working on nonlinear dynamics, complex systems, and network science. My research explores synchronization phenomena and the role of higher-order interactions in complex networks—from neural systems to power grids to social networks.

I'm passionate about understanding how order emerges from chaos, how individual components coordinate to produce collective behavior, and how we can use these insights to solve real-world problems.
