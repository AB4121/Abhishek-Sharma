Publications

Adaptive Kuramoto-Sakaguchi Oscillators in Complex Networks

Abhishek Sharma, [Co-authors]

Journal of Complex Networks • 2025

This paper investigates synchronization dynamics in networks of adaptive Kuramoto-Sakaguchi oscillators. We demonstrate novel phase transitions and provide analytical insights using mean-field theory and numerical simulations.

Finite-size Effects in Kuramoto Oscillators: Phase Transitions and Synchronization

Abhishek Sharma, [Co-authors]

Physical Review E • 2024

A comprehensive study on how finite system sizes affect the synchronization transition in Kuramoto oscillators. We examine scaling laws and finite-size effects in critical behavior for various network topologies.

Read Paper arXiv DOI

Higher-order Interactions in Network Synchronization

Abhishek Sharma, [Co-authors]

International Conference on Complex Systems • 2024

This work explores how higher-order interactions influence collective synchronization behavior. We model systems using hypergraphs and simplicial complexes, revealing mechanisms absent in traditional pairwise networks.

View Abstract Conference Page

Dopamine-modulated Spike-timing-dependent Plasticity in Neural Networks

Abhishek Sharma, [Co-authors]

Neuroscience Research • 2023

Implementation and analysis of dopamine-modulated STDP in spiking neural networks using Brian2. We study learning dynamics and reward-based behavior modification in simulated neural systems.
