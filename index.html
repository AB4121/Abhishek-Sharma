About

I am a PhD scholar at IIT Indore working on nonlinear dynamics, complex systems, and network science. My research focuses on understanding synchronization phenomena and the role of higher-order interactions in complex networks. I combine theoretical analysis with computational modeling to solve problems in network science, neuroscience, and dynamical systems.

With expertise in Python, C++, and advanced computational methods, I develop numerical simulations to validate theoretical predictions and explore novel phenomena in coupled oscillator systems.
